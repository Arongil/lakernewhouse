Principles for Entering MIT

Aug 2021

These principles aim to compress various insights about how to approach college.

  1. Meet many new students.
  2. Capitalize on support networks.
  3. Take classes you're interested in.
  4. Decide on goals before meetings.
  5. Stay grounded in spirit and values.
  6. Stay healthy.
  7. Stay hungry.
  8. Stay kind.
