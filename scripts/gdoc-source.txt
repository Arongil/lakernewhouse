Five Tips for Learning Languages

I taught myself German from scratch using Harry Potter. Here are five tips I can share:

1. Read on a Kindle. I had to look up every word at first, but definitions were just a tap away.
2. Keep at it. The first chapter is the gauntlet, but soon words like Blitznarbe (lightning scar) become old friends.
3. Learn grammar later. After six months of soaking in patterns, I was actually glad to dive into a grammar textbook.
4. Immerse yourself. I added German everywhere in my life, from video games to poetry. 
5. Create output. I wrote awkward short stories using my limited vocabulary, then progressed to talking weekly with a native speaker.

Fun is the secret sauce that makes perseverance effortless. Untethering myself from the trodden path to self-teach German inspires me to stay creative about how I learn everywhere.
